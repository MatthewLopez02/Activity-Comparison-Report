Key Insights & Analysis

Paid Ads Users Show Highest Quality & Transaction Activation

35.08% activation rate for August Paid leads all segments, demonstrating that paid advertising campaigns deliver exceptionally high-quality users. August Organic follows with 30.57%, and October Organic shows 27.87% (revenue + card).

Paid ads users are completing transactions at the highest rate, with more than 1 in 3 users transacting. This validates the effectiveness of our paid acquisition targeting.

Volume & Quality: Paid Ads Deliver Both

Paid campaigns drive 5.8x more volume than organic (24,141 vs 4,141 users in August) while maintaining 15% higher activation rate (35.08% vs 30.57%).

This exceptional performance demonstrates that paid advertising delivers both scale and quality. Paid users represent our highest-value acquisition channel.

Card Activation Drives October Success

October Organic shows 26.16% card activation, which closely aligns with the 27.87% overall activation rate. Card activation is a critical leading indicator for user engagement.

Note: Card activation data not available in AppsFlyer (August data).

P2P Send Leads Revenue Generation

P2P Send is the dominant revenue-generating event across August segments:

  • August Organic: 8.55%
  • August Paid: 3.41%
  • October Organic: 1.85%

While paid users show strong overall activation, organic users demonstrate higher P2P Send adoption, suggesting different usage patterns between acquisition channels.

Cashload Revenue Adoption

Cashload (revenue-generating) shows moderate adoption in August but none in October:

  • August Organic: 5.00%
  • August Paid: 3.24%
  • October Organic: 0.00%

October users haven't had sufficient time to adopt this funding method with associated fees.

Consistent Feature Adoption Across Channels

Crossborder and Ding Voucher adoption rates are relatively consistent between August Organic and Paid users, showing that both channels drive similar product engagement patterns once users activate.

October users show lower adoption (0.02%-0.05%), suggesting these features require longer user tenure before adoption.

Direct Deposit Shows Strongest Commitment in August Organic

August Organic leads in direct deposit adoption with 4.73% of users setting up ACH, demonstrating the highest level of financial commitment across all segments.

  • August Organic: 4.73% (196 users)
  • August Paid: 3.86% (837 users)
  • October Organic: 1.13% (124 users)

Direct deposit is a critical indicator of deep user engagement and long-term retention. August segments show 3-4x higher ACH adoption than October, suggesting users need time to establish this level of financial trust and commitment.
