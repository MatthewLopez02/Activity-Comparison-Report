Key Insights & Analysis

August Organic Leads in Transaction-Based Activation

30.57% activation rate (PRN/transaction-based) for August Organic is the highest across all segments. October Organic follows with 27.87% (revenue + card), while August Paid shows 20.60% activation.

August Organic users demonstrate the strongest transaction behavior, with nearly 1 in 3 users completing at least one transaction.

Volume vs. Quality Trade-off

Paid campaigns drive 5.2x more volume than organic (21,701 vs 4,141 users in August), but at 33% lower activation rate (20.60% vs 30.57%).

Cost-per-activated-user is significantly higher for paid campaigns. Organic users deliver superior transaction rates and better ROI.

Card Activation Drives October Success

October Organic shows 26.16% card activation, which closely aligns with the 27.87% overall activation rate. Card activation is a critical leading indicator for user engagement.

Note: Card activation data not available in AppsFlyer (August data).

P2P Send Leads Revenue Generation

P2P Send is the dominant revenue-generating event across August segments:

  • August Organic: 8.55%
  • August Paid: 3.79%
  • October Organic: 1.85%

October's lower P2P Send rate suggests newer users need more time to reach this revenue milestone.

Cashload Revenue Adoption

Cashload (revenue-generating) shows moderate adoption in August but none in October:

  • August Organic: 5.00%
  • August Paid: 3.61%
  • October Organic: 0.00%

October users haven't had sufficient time to adopt this funding method with associated fees.

Low Crossborder & Ding Voucher Adoption

Crossborder transactions and Ding Voucher purchases show very low adoption in October Organic (0.02% each), but higher rates in August cohorts (Crossborder: 2.61% organic, 1.93% paid).

This suggests these features require longer user tenure before adoption, representing opportunity for targeted campaigns to mature users.
